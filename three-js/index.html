Hello, I'm Benito Correa!

I'm a graduating Computer Engineering student with a curiosity that drives me to understand how things work—and to have fun building them. Whether I'm diving into circuit design, tinkering with embedded systems, or creatively pushing code to its limits, I love the process as much as the result.

My Journey

From the first time I took apart a broken gadget just to explore its parts, to building my own snake-game on an FPGA, the thrill of discovery has always powered my path. In my studies, I’ve dived into digital logic, microcontrollers, and system design—always asking “why” and “how”, and often summoning creative twists that surprise even me.

What I Bring

  • Strong foundations in electronics, digital systems, and firmware development.
  • A tinkerer’s mindset—unafraid to experiment, debug, and learn through hands-on building.
  • Creative problem-solving blended with a structured engineering approach.
  • An enthusiasm for collaboration, learning, and just making cool things work.
